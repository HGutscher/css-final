Development

Treaty 4 has a total of 15 and of those 15 there is only one urban reserve. Their largest reserve in terms of population has 3844 people and the size of 19,142 hectors and is the only urban reserve in Treaty 4. The George Gordon first nation reserve has modernized to the point where it has developed its own phone application. This reserve has an Indian residential school committee, expansion plans for economic growth, community outreach programs, and much more.
